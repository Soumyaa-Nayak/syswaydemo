ABOUT

 
	Falguni Nayar, 
 a former managing director at Kotak Mahindra Capital Company,
 founded Nykaa in 2012.
 It was launched as an ecommerce portal curating a range of beauty 
and wellness products. 
 The brand name Nykaa is derived from the Sanskrit word nayaka,
 meaning 'one in the spotlight'.
